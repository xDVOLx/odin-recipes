Egg Salad

Description

This delicious Egg Salad Sandwich recipe is a hit for picnics, packed lunches, and quick yet satisfying meals. You can easily scale this recipe to fit any number of hungry eaters! As written, this recipe will make approximately 4 egg salad sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • 4 hard boiled eggs
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 pickle spears
  • 1/2 teaspoon yellow mustard
  • paprika, salt, and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Chop hard boiled eggs and pickles into small pieces
  2. Place chopped eggs in a medium-sized mixing bowl
  3. Stir in mayonnaise, pickles, and mustard
  4. Season with paprika, salt, and pepper, as desired
  5. Stir until color is uniform and consistency is palatable
  6. Serve on your favorite bread or crackers
  7. Enjoy!
