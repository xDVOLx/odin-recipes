Hot Chocolate

Description

Whether it's a cold winter day, or you just want a sweet and comforting drink to warm your bones, this recipe for Hot Chocolate will be just what the doctor ordered! As written, this recipe makes approximately 8oz of hot chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons Hershey's cocoa powder
  • 1 cup milk or milk substitute
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • a dash of salt
  • Marshmallows (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix sugar, cocoa, and salt in a large mug
  2. Heat milk in microwave at high for 1 to 1&1/2 minutes or until hot
  3. Gradually add hot milk to cocoa mixture in mug, stirring until well blended
  4. Stir in vanilla extract
  5. Top with marshmallows if desired
  6. Enjoy!
